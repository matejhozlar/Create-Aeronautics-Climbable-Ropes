## Version 1.2.0

### Added
- Added the ability to climb plunger ropes — the rope connections made by the grappling plunger item from Simulated. Right-click the rope with an empty hand to grab on, then use W/S to climb, Sprint+S to slide, Shift to dismount, and Space to jump off. Works on assembled Create contraptions too.
- Sliding down ropes with Sprint+S now ramps up to speed smoothly and coasts to a stop when you release Sprint, instead of snapping instantly between speeds. Maximum slide speed is significantly faster than before.

### Fixed
- Fixed Sprint+S on a near-horizontal plunger rope incorrectly triggering the slide ramp. Descent on shallow ropes now behaves the same as normal descent.
